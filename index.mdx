It brings together a full suite of AI agents — covering everything from patient calls and appointment scheduling to real-time transcription, medical coding, and clinical insights — so your team can focus on what matters most: patient care.

## How to Get Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request early access">
    Visit the [early access page](/clinics/early-access) and submit your request. Futurum is currently in limited access while we scale.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your practice">
    Once approved, follow the [clinic quickstart](/clinics/getting-started) to connect your EHR, configure your AI agents, and onboard your team.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable AI agents">
    Activate the agents your practice needs — Receptionist, Scribe, Coder, and more — from your clinic dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="See results from day one">
    Your team starts saving hours on documentation and administration immediately, with no disruption to existing workflows.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Futurum is currently accepting early access requests for clinics and individual providers. [Request access](https://futurumhealth.ai/early-access/) to get started.
</Note>
