Futurum Nursing AI handles the structured, repeatable parts of nursing workflows — patient intake, vitals review, pre-assessment coordination, and care hand-offs — so your nurses can dedicate their attention to the clinical tasks that require a human touch.

## Key Responsibilities

Nursing AI takes ownership of the workflow steps that consume time without requiring direct clinical judgment:

- **Patient intake** — collects and organizes patient information automatically at the start of each visit, so nothing is missing when care begins
- **Vitals review** — receives vitals from connected devices and flags any out-of-range values for immediate clinical attention
- **Care coordination** — manages workflow transitions between reception, clinical staff, and physicians, keeping every hand-off clean and on time
- **Pre-assessment support** — prepares the complete clinical record before the physician enters the room, so the consultation can begin without delay

## How It Fits Your Workflow

Here is how Nursing AI moves a patient through your clinic from the moment they arrive to the moment they leave.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Patient Checks In">
    Nursing AI collects intake information automatically as soon as the patient arrives, capturing everything your team needs before the visit begins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Vitals Are Recorded and Reviewed">
    Vitals from connected devices are received and automatically reviewed for out-of-range values, with flagged readings surfaced for clinical staff immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pre-Assessment Data Is Merged">
    Health data collected by the Health Chronicler before the visit is merged with intake information to form a single, unified patient summary.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patient Summary Is Ready">
    A complete patient summary — intake details, vitals, and pre-assessment context — is prepared and waiting before the physician enters the room.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Post-Visit Coordination Is Handled">
    After the encounter, Nursing AI manages post-visit care coordination tasks automatically, keeping follow-up workflows on track without manual intervention.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Works Alongside

Nursing AI is designed to operate as part of a connected team of AI agents. It pairs directly with:

- [AI Receptionist](/clinics/ai-receptionist) — hands off patient information from check-in into the nursing workflow seamlessly
- [Clinical Insights AI](/clinics/ai-clinical-insights) — passes the prepared patient summary to Clinical Insights so the physician enters with full analytical context

<Tip>
  Nursing AI keeps care workflows moving even during peak hours — no additional staff, no overtime, and no bottlenecks at intake.
</Tip>
