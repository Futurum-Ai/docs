Futurum Clinical Insights AI analyzes your patient's data — symptoms, vitals, history, and visit context — and generates AI-powered clinical intelligence during the consultation. It flags diagnostic possibilities based on symptom patterns, cross-references patient history with current complaints, and produces shareable clinical reports so your team makes decisions backed by complete information.

## What It Analyzes

Clinical Insights draws on every layer of available patient data to build a comprehensive picture before and during the encounter:

- **Current visit symptoms and findings** — captured in real time from the active consultation
- **Patient medical history and prior visits** — surfaced from the existing clinical record
- **Vitals from connected devices** — pulled automatically from any integrated monitoring equipment
- **Pre-visit assessment data** — collected by Pre-Assessment AI before the patient enters the room

## Example Scenario

A patient presents with recurring headaches. Clinical Insights cross-references their sleep data, activity levels, and meal patterns from the Health Chronicler to surface hypoglycemia as a likely contributing factor — information you might not have had access to otherwise. The insight appears during the consultation, giving you the opportunity to investigate before the patient leaves the room.
