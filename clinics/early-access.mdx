If you want to be part of building the future of clinical AI — not just using it — this is where to start.

## How to Apply

Submit your early access request in a few quick steps.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the early access page">
    Go to [futurumhealth.ai/early-access](https://futurumhealth.ai/early-access/) to find the official request form.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Fill out the form with your name and email">
    Enter your full name and the email address you want associated with your Futurum account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm you can provide feedback">
    Indicate that you are willing and able to provide feedback during the early access program. This is a core part of what makes the program valuable for both sides.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit your request">
    Submit the form. The Futurum team will follow up directly with next steps for getting you onboarded.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Early access users must acknowledge that Futurum is an early-stage AI system designed to support clinical workflows and does not replace professional medical judgment.
</Note>
