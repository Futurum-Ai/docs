Futurum Health AI is an agentic AI platform built for clinics that want to reclaim time and deliver better care. It handles patient calls, visit documentation, and billing end-to-end — so your staff can focus on patients instead of paperwork. From the moment a patient books an appointment to the moment a claim is submitted, Futurum's AI agents work in the background so your team doesn't have to.

## Set Up Your Clinic

Follow these steps to get your clinic live on Futurum Health AI.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Request early access">
    Futurum is currently available to a limited group of early adopters. Visit the [Early Access](/clinics/early-access) page and submit your request to secure your clinic's spot before the public launch.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download the app and create your clinic account">
    Download the Futurum Health AI app and sign up for your clinic account. During setup, add your practice details, configure your staff roles, and invite your team members to join your clinic workspace.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your EHR system">
    Link Futurum to your Electronic Health Record (EHR) system so AI-generated notes, charts, and billing codes sync automatically. Futurum pushes finished documentation directly into your EHR — no copy-pasting, no delays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enable AI agents for your practice">
    Activate the AI agents your clinic needs. You can enable the full suite or turn on individual agents — AI Receptionist, AI Scribe, AI Coder, Clinical Insights, and Nursing AI — based on where your team needs the most support.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## The Impact on Your Practice

Futurum is built to give your team measurable time and energy back from day one.

- **Increased efficiency** — serve more patients without adding staff or extending hours.
- **Daily admin hours saved per clinician** — less time in front of screens means more time in front of patients.
- **Significant decrease in burnout** — when AI handles documentation, billing, and phone calls, your team goes home when patients do.
