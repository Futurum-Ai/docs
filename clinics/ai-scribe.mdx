Futurum AI Scribe listens to your patient visits in real time and converts every conversation into structured clinical documentation. It transcribes medical terminology accurately, identifies key symptoms, structures everything into proper SOAP format, and pushes finished notes directly into your EHR — all before the patient has left the room.

## How It Works

Follow these steps to see how AI Scribe takes a visit from first word to finished chart.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start a Visit — AI Scribe Begins Listening Automatically">
    Open a visit in your dashboard and AI Scribe activates immediately. There's nothing to configure or switch on mid-consultation — it's ready when you are.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Conduct Your Consultation Naturally — No Special Commands Needed">
    Talk to your patient the way you always do. AI Scribe works in the background without interrupting your flow, capturing everything without requiring any special phrases or pauses.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Generates SOAP Notes in Real Time During the Visit">
    Watch your documentation build as the conversation progresses. By the time you wrap up your assessment, the structured note is already taking shape — no post-visit catch-up required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and Approve the Note Before It's Pushed to Your EHR">
    Check the generated SOAP note in your dashboard, make any edits or annotations, and approve it with a single action. You stay in control of every note before it becomes part of the record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Documentation Is Complete — Move Straight to Your Next Patient">
    Once approved, the note pushes directly to your EHR. Your day ends when your last patient leaves — not two hours later at your desk catching up on charts.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Clinicians using AI Scribe reclaim significant time each day — documentation finishes the moment the patient leaves the room.
</Tip>
