Futurum integrates directly with your existing Electronic Health Record (EHR) system, pushing completed clinical notes, SOAP documentation, and billing codes automatically. Your staff never has to manually copy information between systems — every visit ends with a fully populated EHR record.

## What syncs automatically

When a visit concludes, Futurum pushes the complete clinical record to your EHR without any manual intervention. The following data flows automatically from Futurum into your records system:

- **SOAP notes** generated by AI Scribe from the ambient visit recording
- **Clinical charts** structured and formatted by Charting AI for immediate EHR submission
- **ICD and CPT codes** extracted and validated by AI Medical Coder
- **Pre-assessment data** collected by the Health Chronicler before the patient arrived
- **Vitals and intake information** captured and organized by Nursing AI

Every data point is tied to the patient record, the visit timestamp, and the responsible provider — giving you a complete, traceable clinical record with no re-entry required.

## How to connect your EHR

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Integrations in your Futurum dashboard">
    Navigate to **Settings → Integrations** in your Futurum clinic dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your EHR system">
    Choose your EHR from the supported systems list. If you do not see your system, contact [info@futurumhealth.ai](mailto:info@futurumhealth.ai) to ask about compatibility.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Authorize the connection">
    Enter your EHR credentials to authorize Futurum to read and write patient data on your behalf. Futurum uses encrypted, authenticated connections — your credentials are never stored in plain text.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Map your clinical fields">
    Match your EHR's data fields to Futurum's clinical data structure. Futurum provides a guided field-mapping interface to make this straightforward.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Run a test visit">
    Complete a test visit and confirm that SOAP notes, codes, and clinical data appear correctly in your EHR. Adjust field mappings if needed before going live.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  Futurum is building toward global EMR integration. Contact [info@futurumhealth.ai](mailto:info@futurumhealth.ai) to ask about your specific EHR system.
</Note>

## Data flow and security

Every data transmission between Futurum and your EHR travels over an encrypted connection with a full audit log. You can see exactly what was pushed, when it was pushed, and which provider's visit triggered it — giving you a clear record for compliance reviews and discrepancy investigations.

<Info>
  All data transmitted between Futurum and your EHR is end-to-end encrypted and HIPAA-compliant.
</Info>
