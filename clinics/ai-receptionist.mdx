## How It Works

Follow these steps to see how a typical patient interaction flows from first ring to ready-to-see.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Patient Calls Your Clinic's Number">
    The call reaches AI Receptionist instantly — no hold queue, no voicemail. It's available around the clock, including evenings, weekends, and holidays.
  </Step>
  <Step title="AI Receptionist Answers and Handles the Request Naturally">
    The AI engages the patient in natural conversation, identifying whether they need to book an appointment, ask a question, verify insurance, or complete intake — then acts on it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Appointment Is Scheduled and Confirmed in Your Calendar">
    AI Receptionist checks your real-time availability, books the appointment, and sends the patient a confirmation — all within the same call, with no staff involvement required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Patient Intake Information Is Collected and Ready Before the Visit">
    Before the patient arrives, their health intake details are captured and waiting in your dashboard. Your care team walks in with the full picture, every time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Always On, Always Ready

AI Receptionist is available 24/7 — it never puts a patient on hold, never calls in sick, and never falls behind on a busy morning. Every inbound call gets answered with the same quality and consistency, whether it comes in at 9 AM or 2 AM.

<Note>
  AI Receptionist works alongside your existing staff, handling routine calls so your team can focus on patients already in the office.
</Note>
