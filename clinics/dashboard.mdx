The Futurum clinic dashboard is your command center. It surfaces everything happening in your practice in real time — patient flow, staff schedules, room occupancy, wait times, and compliance status — so you can act immediately rather than scramble for information.

## Dashboard sections

Your dashboard is organized into five purpose-built sections, each giving you a live view of a different layer of your practice operations.

### Patient Flow

See which patients are checked in, actively being seen, or still waiting. Patient Flow makes bottlenecks visible the moment they start to form — before wait times spiral and before your staff is overwhelmed. Use it to keep your patient pipeline moving without constant manual check-ins.

### Staff Schedules

Monitor provider and staff availability in real time. Staff Schedules shows you who is on duty, who is with a patient, and where coverage gaps are opening up. When a team member is running behind, you see it immediately and can act before it cascades.

### Room Status

Know at a glance which rooms are occupied, empty, or being cleaned. Room Status eliminates the back-and-forth between front desk and clinical staff, and helps you assign incoming patients to available rooms without delay.

### Wait Times

Track patient wait times live across your entire practice. Wait Times gives you the data to set accurate expectations with patients and to intervene before frustration builds. When one provider's queue stretches, you can see it and redistribute load in real time.

### Compliance Status

Futurum continuously monitors your HIPAA documentation requirements and generates a real-time audit trail for every patient interaction. Compliance Status surfaces any gaps in documentation or access control so you can address them immediately — not during an audit.

<Note>
  The dashboard updates in real time — there is no refresh needed. Every patient interaction, room change, and schedule update is reflected instantly.
</Note>

## Scale from 3 providers to 300

The Futurum dashboard is built to grow with your practice. Whether you run a three-physician clinic or a multi-site enterprise with hundreds of providers, a single dashboard surfaces the metrics that matter across your entire operation — without slowdowns, data loss, or system complexity.

## Roles and permissions

Every team member sees the view that fits their role. Futurum applies role-based permissions so each staff member gets the metrics relevant to their work — and nothing outside their scope.

| Role | What they see |
|---|---|
| **Front Desk** | Patient check-in status, appointment queue, wait times |
| **Clinical Staff** | Room status, patient flow, vitals and intake summaries |
| **Billing** | Coding status, completed visit documentation, billing flags |
| **Admin** | Full practice overview, compliance status, staff schedules |

Role-based views reduce information overload and keep sensitive data accessible only to authorized personnel.
