Futurum's Health Chronicler connects to the health data sources you already use — Apple Health, Google Fit, wearables, and medical devices — and aggregates them into one complete health profile. Your doctor gets a full picture of your health between visits, not just what you remember to mention.

## Supported integrations

Futurum connects to the most widely used consumer health platforms and a broad range of personal medical devices:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Apple Health / Apple Watch" icon="apple" href="/patients/wearable-integrations#how-to-connect-a-data-source">
    Syncs heart rate, sleep data, activity, ECG readings, and any other health metrics you've enabled in Apple Health.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Google Fit" icon="google" href="/patients/wearable-integrations#how-to-connect-a-data-source">
    Pulls in steps, activity, and heart rate data from your Google Fit account and any devices connected to it.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Wearables" icon="watch" href="/patients/wearable-integrations#how-to-connect-a-data-source">

  <Card title="Medical devices" icon="heart-pulse" href="/patients/wearable-integrations#how-to-connect-a-data-source">
    Blood pressure monitors, glucose monitors, and pulse oximeters that connect to your phone and report to Apple Health or Google Fit sync automatically.

## How to connect a data source

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings → Devices">
    Open the Futurum app, tap your profile, and navigate to **Settings → Devices**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your data source">
    Choose the platform or device you want to connect — Apple Health, Google Fit, or a specific medical device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Grant permissions when prompted">
    Your phone will ask you to authorize Futurum to read data from the selected source. Grant the permissions you're comfortable with — you choose exactly which data types are shared.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Futurum begins aggregating your data immediately">
    Once you grant permissions, the Health Chronicler starts pulling in your health data right away. No additional setup required.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm data is flowing">
    Go to the **Health Overview** tab to verify your connected sources are active and data is being received correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What data is synced

Once a source is connected, Futurum continuously aggregates the following data types into your health profile:

| Data type | What's tracked |
|---|---|
| **Heart rate** | Resting heart rate, active heart rate, and continuous readings where available |
| **Sleep** | Sleep duration, quality indicators, and patterns over time |
| **Activity** | Daily steps, exercise sessions, and general movement |
| **Additional vitals** | Blood pressure, blood glucose, oxygen saturation, and other readings from connected medical devices |

This data feeds directly into your pre-visit reports, so your doctor receives an accurate, continuous view of your health — not just a self-reported snapshot from the day of the appointment.

<Note>
  You control which data sources are connected and can disconnect any source at any time from **Settings → Devices**.
</Note>

<Info>
  Data from connected devices is encrypted at rest and in transit. Futurum never sells your health data to third parties.
</Info>
