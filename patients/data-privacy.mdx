Your health data belongs to you. Futurum is built with HIPAA compliance and end-to-end encryption at its core, so every symptom log, device reading, and doctor interaction is protected at every step. You control what gets shared, with whom, and when.

## How your data is protected

Every layer of the Futurum platform is built with security by design:

- **End-to-end encryption** on all data in transit and at rest — your health information is encrypted from the moment it leaves your device to the moment it's accessed by an authorized party.
- **HIPAA-compliant data storage and access controls** — Futurum meets the full requirements of the Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act for handling protected health information.
- **Role-based access controls** — only authorized parties see your information. Doctors receive only the reports you explicitly send them.
- **Comprehensive audit logs** — every data access event is logged, creating a complete record of who accessed what and when.

## What is never shared without your explicit consent

The following data is yours alone. Futurum does not share it with any party — including your own care team — without a deliberate action on your part:

- Your symptom logs
- Your wearable and device data
- Your pre-visit reports
- Your visit summaries

<Note>
  When you send a pre-visit report to your doctor, only the doctor or clinic you select receives it. Futurum does not share reports automatically.
</Note>
