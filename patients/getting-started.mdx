Getting started with Futurum takes just a few minutes. Once you download the app and create your account, you can connect your Apple Watch, Fitbit, or other health devices and start building the complete health profile your doctor needs. From there, logging symptoms and sending pre-visit reports is just a tap away.

## Set Up Your Account

Follow these steps to go from download to your first health report.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download the Futurum app">
    Get Futurum from the **App Store** (iOS) or **Google Play** (Android) and install it on your phone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create your patient account">
    Open the app and sign up using your name and email address. Your account is secured and HIPAA-compliant from the moment you create it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Connect your wearables and health devices">
    Link your Apple Health, Google Fit, or compatible medical devices so Futurum can start tracking your data automatically. See the full list of supported integrations at [Wearable Integrations](/patients/wearable-integrations).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log your first symptoms">
    Tap the symptom log and speak or type in plain language — no medical terms needed. Describe what you're feeling in your own words and Futurum's AI structures it for your health profile.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Send your health report to your doctor">
    Before your next appointment, send your doctor a complete pre-visit health report with one tap. Learn more at [Pre-Visit Reports](/patients/pre-visit-reports).
  </Step>
</Steps>

## What You Can Do Right Away

As soon as your account is set up and your devices are connected, you have immediate access to the following:

- **Log symptoms in your own words** — use voice or text, no medical terminology required
- **View your daily health overview** — see your heart rate, sleep duration, and step count at a glance
- **Track patterns over time** — watch how your health metrics trend across days and weeks
- **Send a report to your doctor** — share your complete health summary before any appointment

<Tip>
  Logging symptoms daily — even when you feel fine — gives your doctor a more complete picture of your baseline health. The more context Futurum has, the more useful your pre-visit reports become.
</Tip>

## Your Privacy

Your health data belongs to you. Futurum is built on full HIPAA compliance with end-to-end encryption and strict access controls. You decide what gets shared and with whom. Learn more at [Data Privacy](/patients/data-privacy).
