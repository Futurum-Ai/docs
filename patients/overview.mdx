## Why This Matters

The traditional appointment model puts enormous pressure on a very short window of time. Here's what that means for your care — and how Futurum addresses each gap.

| The Problem | What It Means for You |
|---|---|
| **Appointments average 10–20 minutes** | There isn't enough time to cover everything happening between visits |
| **Recall bias** | You remember the major symptoms but forget patterns — when things started, what made them worse, how your sleep or activity changed |
| **Incomplete data** | Your doctor makes decisions based on what you remember to say, not the full picture |

Futurum solves all three. Your doctor gets complete health context from every source — your wearables, your symptom logs, your medical devices — always up to date, always accessible before they see you.
