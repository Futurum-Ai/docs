Futurum's Health Chronicler is an AI agent that aggregates your health data continuously — from your wearables, medical devices, and your own symptom logs — into one complete health profile. Between appointments, it's building the context your doctor needs to make informed decisions at your next visit. You don't have to summarize weeks of symptoms from memory; Futurum has already tracked every detail.

## Logging Symptoms in Your Own Words

You don't need to know medical terminology to log a symptom. Speak or type in plain language and Futurum's AI understands what you mean, then structures it into a medically useful format.

Here's how that works in practice:

<Info>
  **What you say:**
  "I've been having headaches for the past week, usually in the afternoon. They feel worse when I skip lunch."

  **What Futurum extracts:**
  - **Symptom:** Recurring headaches
  - **Duration:** 7 days
  - **Pattern:** Afternoon onset
  - **Trigger:** Meal timing
</Info>

That structured entry becomes part of the health report your doctor reviews before your next appointment — no important details lost, no recall required.

## Your Daily Health Overview

Every day, Futurum gives you an at-a-glance summary of your key health metrics so you can see how you're doing and spot trends early.

| Metric | What You See |
|---|---|
| **Avg BPM** | Your average heart rate for the day |
| **Sleep** | Total sleep duration in hours |
| **Steps** | Your daily step count |
| **Trends** | Indicators showing whether each metric is improving, stable, or declining |

<Note>
  The Health Chronicler runs in the background automatically once you connect your devices. You don't need to do anything special — it aggregates data continuously so your health profile is always current when you need it.
</Note>
