Amazon Bedrock delivers the AI and infrastructure layer that Futurum is built on. You benefit from its capabilities without managing any of it directly.

## Reliability

Futurum's AI agents run on infrastructure designed for continuous, uninterrupted operation.

- **24/7 availability** — AI agents never go offline and never take breaks
- **Automatic scaling** during high-volume periods such as flu season or appointment rushes
- **No infrastructure management** required from your team — Futurum handles all hosting, scaling, updates, and maintenance

## Data Residency and Encryption

All patient data processed by Futurum is handled on HIPAA-eligible AWS infrastructure, with the following protections enforced by default:

- Fully encrypted at rest
- Fully encrypted in transit
- Data processed exclusively on HIPAA-eligible AWS infrastructure

## Performance

Futurum is built to keep pace with clinical workflows in real time.

- **Real-time transcription** during patient visits — no post-processing delay
- **Instant clinical insights generation** as visit data is captured
- **Synchronous EHR data push** on visit completion — notes reach the EHR the moment the visit ends

<Note>
